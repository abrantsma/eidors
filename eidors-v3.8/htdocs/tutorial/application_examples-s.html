Application Examples

To run these tutorials, you need to download and install EIDORS and then run this command in a matlab (or octave) session. 
>>run /path/to/eidors3d/startup.m

Application: EIT of the thorax

  • Lung recruitment in ARDS (human) [data from Wolf et al (2012)]

  • PEEP trial after lung injury (pig) [data from Frerichs et al (2003)]

  • Changes in ventilation after lung injury (pig) [data from Frerichs et al (1998)]

  • Images of neonate lungs [data from Heinrich et al (2006)]

  • Images of lungs (Human shallow breathing)

  • Images of stomach emptying

Application: EIT for geophysics

  • Electrical images of Pont Péan region

  • EIT of borehole

  • Simulation of EIT in a tunnel

  • Simulation of 3D surface electrode system

  • Geophysical stimulation patterns aud pseudo-section representation
